      RSS and Atom Feeds

      Yelp now offers Really Simple Syndication (RSS) and Atom feeds for the hardcore Yelper. If you haven't heard of RSS or Atom you might want to check out the BBC's What is RSS? page or read up on Atom.

      What can I do with these?

      • Track recent reviews in your newsreader.
      • Publish reviews to your blog or another feed-enabled website (like Facebook).
      • Send your RSS or Atom link to friends so they can keep tabs on your latest reviews.
