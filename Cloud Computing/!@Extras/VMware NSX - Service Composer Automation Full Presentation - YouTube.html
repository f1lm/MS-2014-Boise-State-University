    In this video, we will see how the NSX Service Composer automates the process of applying security policy to VM instances in real time. This video includes a short presentation that introduces NSX Service Composer Automation and describes the use case presented in the demo. Following the presentation, a complete demonstration of NSX Service Composer Automation is included, which shows how the NSX Service Composer automatically detects and quarantines a VM that has become infected, remediates the infection and subsequently restores the previously infected VM to service.
