What Is dashDB?

Say hello to IBM dashDB™

IBM dashDB is a powerful, agile data warehousing solution on the cloud that that puts an analytics powerhouse at your fingertips. The next wave in data warehousing is here. dashDB allows you to break free from the bonds of infrastructure when your business demands it.

dashDB can help extend your existing infrastructure into the cloud, or help you start new data warehousing self-service capabilities. It is powered by high performance in-memory and in-database technology that delivers answers as fast as you can think. dashDB provides the simplicity of an appliance with the elasticity and agility of the cloud for any size organization. Security? Yes we thought of that too, dashDB is designed to meet your expectations of enterprise security.

This changes everything—a data warehouse that moves as fast as your business!

  • Build more
    Keep the infrastructure of data warehousing out of your way. dashDB is simple to get up and running in minutes with rapid cloud provisioning. Create more value in your business without capital expenditures. Whether it’s enabling new analytic capabilities, answering questions faster, or being able to capitalize on a short term opportunity ahead of the competition, you can easily build the capabilities that deliver answers.
  • Grow more
    Focus on growing your business, not the business of data warehousing. Leverage next-generation in-memory processing, columnar data stores optimized for analytics, and load and go simplicity to deliver critical answers as fast as you can think of the next question. A “waiting for results” coffee break? Not required.
  • Know more
    Be first in your industry to capitalize on opportunities. The power to unlock your data is at your fingertips with dashDB. Built-in analytics, R predictive modeling and an ecosystem of partner BI and ETL tools free you to analyze the data to get more precise insights than your competition.
