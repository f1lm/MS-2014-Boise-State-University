Don’t lose your way! Get your map to rethinking IT service delivery!

More Sharing Services

3 Comments
1

Guest Post by Sherry Ramm, ITSM Marketing Consultant

 

Wouldn’t it be great if you had a single picture that showed, in a nutshell, the WHYs and HOWs to become an IT Service Broker? Well, today is your lucky day. Click here to view a dcoument that gives you a comprehensive view. If you love using, posting and sharing intelligent, consise visuals that give you 1,000 words in a single view, get yours now.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Download this comprehensive infographic that shows:

  • Why IT needs to rethink IT service delivery
  • Why the time is now
  • How to get there

 

“If you’re not planning or have not implemented self-service, start now.” -- Forrester

Whether you’re in the planning stages or are already on the path to consumer-oriented IT service delivery, get a better understanding of your journey here. In this single view,you can get valuable data including:

 

  • What are the three over-arching trends inhibiting IT’s ability to deliver? See the breakdown and get the concrete stats behind trends, such as:
    • By 2017, 75 percent of business processes will include software as a service.
    • BYOD (bring your own device) 29 percent of workers use three or more devices
    • 85 percent of line-of-business departments use non-approved applications
    • 50 percent of CEOs expect more strategic value from IT.
  • Understand the five disciplines that allow the IT Service Broker to regain control and build influence.
  • See the three-part model consisting of best-of-breed technologies to enable the IT Service Broker.

 

“The highest level of service catalog is the service broker model, or strategic control point. Highly automated, the catalog balances supply and demand, and tools span cloud and noncloud resources.” --Forrester
