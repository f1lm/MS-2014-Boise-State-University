                        What about precedent? It's good to look at the history of such agreements. These agreements aren't static documents in the internet age. They are routinely updated and changed and while companies notify users of changes and in principle you can handle data in a way that isn't "evil", in practice that guarantee can be quite weak. You can't predict the future with 100% certainty, but you can follow trends to make reasonable predictions or at least know what you ought to watch out for. No exception here.
