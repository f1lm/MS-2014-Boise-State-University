WEBINAR
Understanding the difference between Microsoft Azure and Amazon AWS
November 6, 10:30 am MST

Businesses are struggling with the notion of moving to the public cloud, and it’s not surprising. It’s very easy to size on premises data centers; you’ve likely been doing it for 20 years.

But now, you are facing a tough job. There’s a lot to consider when taking your on premises data centers and integrating with the public cloud. What types of conversions do you need to do in order to leverage the public cloud? How do you size to the public cloud? How can you see through the hype and marketing behind both Microsoft Azure and Amazon AWS?

In this webinar, Pluralsight author Elias Khnaser walks listeners through some of the basic pain points and inhibitors associated with moving from the technology you’ve already mastered toward a brand new infrastructure.
