Machine Learning has been around for well over a decade and, with the rise of “Big Data” analytics, has become especially prominent in the last few years.  Many companies are now considering how best to use Machine Learning for predictive and prescriptive analytics and, ultimately, for maximizing revenue, profit and efficiency.

But Machine Learning has not seen broad adoption by mainstream technology specialists, and certainly not by business users.  That’s a shame, because ML technology is evermore developer-friendly and, for it to be deployed most effectively, business users need to understand reasonable use cases for ML in their organizations.

This Webinar will explore Machine Learning concepts and use cases in terms business users can understand. The panel will also discuss in concrete terms how Machine Learning can be used by mainstream developers and database professionals.

Join Gigaom Research and our sponsor Microsoft for “Machine Learning for business users and enterprise developers,” a free analyst webinar on Thursday, February 12, 2015 at 10 a.m. PT.

 Will Be Discussed:

              •  Major machine learning algorithms and groupings

              •  Typical use cases for ML, and industries in which is it most widely-used

              •  Areas where ML is used less often, but are use cases with great efficacy, nevertheless

              •  The differences between data mining, machine learning, deep learning and predictive analytics

              •  How to integrate ML functionality into line-of-business applications
