Introducing the IEEE ComSoc White Paper Library

2
Need insights and innovative ideas? Explore the IEEE ComSoc White Paper Library as we connect you with relevant knowledge and information from the experts. We're happy to help you stay competitive in today's ever more challenging communications field. 
 
The IEEE ComSoc White Paper Library provides free vendor-supplied latest technical content in Communications field on an array of topics. From standards to cloud computing, from mobility to open source platforms, the IEEE ComSoc White Paper Library helps you stay at the forefront of the evolving world of technology. 
 
Current White Papers available for download include:
 
GaN PA Supports 3.5-3.7 GHz Small Cell Applications Using Digital Predistortion
 
Simple Method of Changing the Frequency Range of a Power Amplifier Circuit – Application Note from Freescale
 
100G+ on a Standard Platform
 
Digital Broadcast Video Streaming – technical challenges and solutions
 
Virtual Video Transcoding in the Cloud
 
 
 
Visit the IEEE ComSoc White Paper Library at: http://www.comsoc.org/whitepapers
