CS Graduate Defense Form

This form is intended to guide you through the final phases of defending your thesis. there are a number of small considerations that typically escape the attention of a student in the final weeks before they defend. Please consider the items on this form about a month prior to your defense and submit this to the department at least two weeks prior to your defense.

Once submitted your information will be sent to the department office and someone will contact you with details regarding your room reservation and flyer.
